Platform Pricing

Vorto Platform offers a different way for game developers to generate revenue from their games. With Vorto Platform and service, game developers can create tokenized in-game items to sell to their players.

Compared to other game monetization mechanisms, tokenized in-game items (NFTs) offer a compelling alternative with a lower service cut and leave more control for the developers to involve their players and communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the platform fee?

Vorto charges a platform fee on all sales on the platform to cover operating and development costs of the platform and services, and payment processing costs.

Marketplace sales

For marketplace sales, the platform fee is charged from the seller. Currently Vorto platform fee for marketplace sales is _%. Developers can set their own royalty rate for marketplace sales, that a seller would be charged, in addition to the platform fee.

Royalty rates

Developers can set their own royalty rate for their created items from marketplace sales on the Vorto platform. On some blockchains, it’s possible to assign a royalty rate on subsequent sales that happen on-chain. On-chain royalty rates need to be defined when setting up the smart contract before creating (minting) on-chain items.
