Consumable or Tokenized?

Vorto platform supports two distinct item types. Games can create tradeable consumable items (like resource items for crafting) that typically are used in bulk, or tokenize specific in-game items (like hero characters or valuable equipment). Tokenized items can be either created on the Vorto platform or as NFTs on a supported blockchain.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Vorto Account?

Vorto accounts allow players to hold and connect their (earned or purchased) in-game digital inventory all in one place. With a Vorto account, you can play all games compatible with the Vorto games network (“The Playground” ) and have direct access to your usable in-game items through a single integrated sign-on.

Why do I need another account?

Vorto accounts track your tokenized game items and make the ownership information and associated asset metadata available through game-play. Vorto game developers can access this information in a simplified and consistent way with supported blockchains and players don’t need to remember what items they have collected across different games and blockchains. It provides a single inventory viewpoint for all your tokenized (NFT) game items across the network.

How to join Vorto as a player?

Click on the Log In button on the Homepage menu and register with Vorto in just a few steps. Verify your email address and start using your Vorto account to play great games on the Playground while accumulating inventory for all your in-game items.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payments for buying, trading and selling are accepted in various Credit & DEbit cards, as well as a range of Crypto currency formats such as USDC. Vorto uses accredited 3rd party payment partners (PSP) solutions across its services.

Can players get a refund?

Players can get a refund only under the applicable laws and regulations, as all sales are final.

How to use in-game items?

You can sell your items and trade them with other players within the Vorto network of players, or you can keep your items in inventory and use them in the game.

How to sell items?

Simply list your items on the Marketplace to sell them. Choose any item from your inventory that you want to sell and click on the Sell button. Pick the price for your item and list it for sale.

How to buy items?

You can buy any available item on the Store or Marketplace by clicking the Buy button. Choose your preferred payment method, and your item will be in your inventory within minutes.

Is Vorto platform free for players?

Creating an account on the Vorto platform is completely free. Using the Vorto marketplace, players will pay a small fee for the selling of their in-game items to cover the cost of sales. In the future Vorto may also develop player related services, but nothing will be mandatory to use.

Is Vorto platform free for game studios?

Vorto is a platform for game Studios to build their web3 capabilities and expose them to players with fun-to-earn games. Vorto takes a small fee on every purchase players make on the official Store, payable by the game studio.
